Introduction

Shubhakshika Educational Society is an NGO dedicated to providing quality education and guidance to underprivileged children. My involvement with this NGO was a fulfilling and enriching experience where I got to engage with children and provide them with career counseling.

Objectives

  • Providing educational support to underprivileged children.
  • Offering career counseling to help shape their future.
  • Engaging with children to inspire confidence and motivate them to pursue their dreams.

Methodology

Target Population

Children from underprivileged backgrounds, primarily those who are part of the Shubhakshika Educational Society.

What Have We Done?

We organized sessions to provide career counseling and engaged with children through educational activities.

Major Problems Faced

  • Lack of exposure to diverse career options.
  • Limited access to resources for pursuing higher education.
  • Challenges in building self-confidence among children.

Results

Throughout my involvement, I gained valuable skills such as effective communication, teaching, and mentoring. The children were receptive, and it was rewarding to see them grow more confident.
